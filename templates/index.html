Your trusted digital healthcare companion

MediSync is an intuitive and seamless medical appointment management system designed to connect patients and doctors effortlessly. Whether you want to book an appointment, manage your medical history, or update your availability as a doctor—MediSync brings everything together in one unified platform.

What can you do with MediSync?

For Patients

✔ Search for doctors by name or specialization
✔ View doctor profiles, qualifications, experience, and available timings
✔ Book appointments instantly based on real-time availability
✔ View, manage, or cancel upcoming appointments
✔ Access your treatment history from past visits
✔ Maintain a personalized medical profile including blood group and medical history

For Doctors

✔ Manage your daily and weekly availability with ease
✔ View all patient appointments in a simple dashboard
✔ Update appointment status (Completed / Cancelled)
✔ Record diagnosis, prescription, and patient notes
✔ Maintain complete patient treatment history through linked appointments

For Admin

✔ Manage all doctors, patients, and appointments in one place
✔ Add, update, or remove doctor profiles
✔ Search and filter records for efficient management
✔ Access a live overview of all system users and their activities
